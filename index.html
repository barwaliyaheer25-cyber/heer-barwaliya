About Me

I am a passionate learner, classical dancer, and educator who believes in creativity, discipline, and continuous growth. My journey has been shaped by performing arts, teaching young learners, and exploring business and entrepreneurship.

I have trained extensively in Bharatanatyam and successfully completed my Arangetram, a milestone representing years of dedication and performance training. Alongside this, I am currently practicing Kathak, further expanding my understanding of rhythm, storytelling, and expression.

I have performed in 30+ group stage performances and represented India internationally in Turkey and Hungary. One of my most memorable experiences was performing in front of Former Chief Minister Vijay Rupani.

Currently, I work as a Playgroup Teacher at Little Millennium School, creating engaging and creative learning environments for young children. I am also pursuing a BBA in Digital Business & Entrepreneurship from IIM Bangalore.
